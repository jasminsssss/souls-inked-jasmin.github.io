Our Purpose & Mission

Souls Inked is not just an online bookstore; it is a sanctuary for bibliophiles, a digital haven where the love for literature converges with curated excellence. We are curators of stories, stewards of words, and custodians of the written art.

At Souls Inked, our mission is clear — to bring the world of literature to your fingertips. We carefully curate a diverse selection of books, ranging from timeless classics to contemporary masterpieces, ensuring that every reader finds a piece of literary art that resonates with their soul.


Join us at Souls Inked, where curated excellence meets the enduring magic of storytelling.

Introducing Souls Inked Bookstore's Owner


Jasmin J. Lingat, the founder of Souls Inked Bookstore, got inspired to start the online bookstore because of her sister's love for books. Jasmin also shares a passion for reading, which led to the creation of Souls Inked.

Motivated by the joy of reading and a desire to bring people together through stories, Jasmin curated a diverse collection of books on Souls Inked. The bookstore is more than just a place to buy books; it's a community where book lovers can connect and celebrate the magic of storytelling.

Her journey with Souls Inked is a tribute to the power of literature and the connections we make through the love of reading. The bookstore is a special space where everyone is welcome to explore, share, and enjoy the world of books.

Jasmin J. Lingat, a first-year student from DCPET 1-3 in the Institute of Technology at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines.
